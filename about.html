About Sam

Hi, I'm a graduate of Computer Games Design, looking towards a career in web development

My eye for polish and an eagerness to deliver websites of a gold standard, as well as an understanding of user patterns and psychographics, knowledge drawn from my university course, are the foundational skills and ideals that inform my approach to web development.
